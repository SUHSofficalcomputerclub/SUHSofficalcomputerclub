Students

We are a group of like minded students who want to better ourselves with all the tools available to us, striving to ever advance our knowledge of computers, their functions, how to use them, and how to best utilize them. Learning only takes a drive and the will to act on that drive, so we take every moment to drive ourselves forward

Dreamers

We take our dreams and make them all they can be, pushing ourselves to the limits to achieve what is needed to better ourselves and our world. One line of code at a time.

Friends

Of Course, we are friends, we work together, play together, eat together, and code together. We appreciate the time we have with each other and make every second count. We are a family.

What we do

What you can expect from the club its members.

Learn to program

All members get hands on experience with computers dedicated to programming and are guided through the process of learning to program

Public Gaming facilities

Many School events will have access to club supported gaming setups carrying classic games to enjoy and pass the time among the other activities

Tech Support

Our members are skilled in many forms of computer hardware and are willing to help assist in any issues you may have, most times being free of charge.

Movies Night Setups

At various times in the year we will have school approved movie nights that all are allowed to attend, donations at the door are highly appreciated.

Connections

In our facilities we have access to real industry software and share it amoung our members for various projects
